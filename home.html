Discover the luxurious Hotel Bliss, where comfort meets elegance.

A precious jewel strung along the Indian Ocean overlooking the historic Galle Face Green, Hotel Bliss celebrates the best of the Colombo city. Find sanctuary in our expansive rooms that blend modern sophistication with stunning views, indulge in world-class cuisine at our award-winning restaurants, discover a shopper's paradise at Hotel Bliss’s own Bliss Shopping Mall or host a picture-perfect event in Sri Lanka’s largest banqueting space.
